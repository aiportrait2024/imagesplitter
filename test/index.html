  • Convenient Design Work: Quickly divide large images into grids or equal parts for layout purposes.

How to Use ?

  1. Upload Your Image: Drag and drop or click to upload.
  2. Choose Splitting Options: Select grid dimensions (e.g., 2x2, 3x3) or custom sizes.
  3. Preview and Adjust: Preview how your image will be divided.
  4. Download Your Files: Save the split images individually or as a zip file.

FAQs

How do I upload an image on the Image Splitter?

Yes, you can use custom dimensions to split images into unequal sections.

Once your image is split, click the "Download" button to save the parts.

Yes, your images are securely processed and deleted after splitting.
